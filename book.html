About the Book

More Than a Photography Book — It's a Love Letter to Atlanta

Through stunning images, Desirée James captures the resilience, soul, and powerful stories woven into Atlanta's skyline and streets. This is the city as she has lived it — from its golden sunrises over Piedmont Park to its sacred civil rights landmarks, from its electric jazz culture to its community in motion.

A photojournalist, street photographer, and storyteller — Desirée James spent years walking Atlanta's neighborhoods, attending its gatherings, and listening to its rhythms. This book is the culmination of that devotion.
