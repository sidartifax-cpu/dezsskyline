Meet Desiree
James

Her Story

It's not an online destination to reach — it's a journey of discovery that nourishes and supports the souls of peace-lovers, peacemakers, and those who have chosen to be the catalyst of change. Here you will find the landscape of a vision created from the past, centered in the present moments now, with a foresight and the clear vision of the future.

"A storyteller, and a Renaissance woman."

Desiree James' creativity is a calling. Her roots originate from the oxbow bend along the Bayou Teche, New Iberia, Louisiana. Her gift of artistry is vast, and it spans many decades. From her velvety voice, her creative gifts have flourished as a skilled and highly sought-after voice artist, acclaimed radio show host, musicologist, and photographer.

Since 1987, Desiree James has resided in Atlanta — building community, capturing beauty, and telling the stories that too often go untold. She is a promoter of unconditional love, empowerment of others, and sisterhood. Wherever she lands, you'll discover a peaceful, loving, and supportive community.
